OPhO

Largest Online Student
Run Physics Olympiad

Get ready for our team-based online physics olympiad! This week long
contest will be held from May 16th to May 23rd and will cover topics
from mechanics, electromagnetism, thermodynamics, relativity, waves,
and more. The problems will start off easy and become harder towards the end.

Sign-ups will begin a month prior. Check back soon for more updates!

Format

Work together in teams of up to three on 30+ challenging physics problems in a week. All answers are numerical and points will be awarded for both speed and accuracy. Top teams will be given prizes!

Complete rules will be posted a month prior to the competition.
